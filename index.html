Laboratory

The IFSP CodeLab is a Software Development Laboratory dedicated to teaching and learning. Our goal is to create an environment that fosters the development of students in the courses of Technical High School in Computing and Technology in Analysis and Systems Development.

Our mission is to provide students with a practical and realistic experience in software development, aligned with the demands of the job market. Through this approach, they can apply the theoretical knowledge gained in the classroom and develop both their technical and behavioral skills.

At IFSP CodeLab, we value the interaction between theory and practice, encouraging students to explore various technologies and development methodologies. Additionally, we promote collaboration and teamwork, as we believe these skills are essential for success in the field of technology.

With an experienced and dedicated faculty, we offer individualized support and guidance to students, assisting them in their growth and development as software professionals.
